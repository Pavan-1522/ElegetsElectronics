Agri-Tech IoT

Affordable Agri-Sensor Network

Problem Statement: Indian small-hold farmers lack access to affordable technology for monitoring soil health (NPK, moisture) and micro-climates, leading to inefficient water/fertilizer use and lower crop yields.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths: In-house expertise in low-power embedded systems.
Weaknesses: Lack of established distribution channels in rural areas.
Opportunities: Massive untapped market; government subsidies for Agri-Tech.
Threats: Competition from established players; hardware durability in harsh conditions.

Health Tech Wearables

Remote Vitals Monitor for Elderly Care

Problem Statement: Families in Tier-2/3 cities need a low-cost, easy-to-use wearable to remotely monitor vital signs (SpO2, heart rate, fall detection) of elderly relatives, sending alerts to caregivers' phones.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths: Strong PCB design and firmware development capabilities.
Weaknesses: Navigating medical device regulations can be complex and slow.
Opportunities: Growing aging population; increasing smartphone penetration for caregiver apps.
Threats: Data privacy concerns; competition from Chinese manufacturers.

EV Tech Automotive

Advanced EV Battery Management System

Problem Statement: The burgeoning Indian 2-wheeler EV market requires a robust, locally-developed Battery Management System (BMS) focused on thermal management, safety, and optimizing battery lifespan for local conditions.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths: Expertise in power electronics and controller design.
Weaknesses: High R&D investment required; dependency on imported battery cells.
Opportunities: "Make in India" initiative; massive growth in the EV scooter/motorcycle market.
Threats: Rapidly changing battery chemistries; established international BMS suppliers.
